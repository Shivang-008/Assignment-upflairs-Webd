Biography: A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Early Life

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was born on 15th October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. Coming from a modest background, he was deeply interested in science and spent hours studying physics and aerospace engineering.

Achievements

Known as the "Missile Man of India", Dr. Kalam played a key role in India's civilian space and military missile programs. He later became the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. His humility, vision, and dedication continue to inspire millions.

He also authored many books like "Wings of Fire" and "Ignited Minds", which remain bestsellers. For more information, visit his Wikipedia page

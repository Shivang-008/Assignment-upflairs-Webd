My 60 Memorable Games

"My 60 Memorable Games" is a classic chess book by Grandmaster Bobby Fischer . It presents 60 of Fischer’s most important and instructive games, annotated with deep insights and personal commentary. A masterpiece of strategic thought, the book is celebrated for its clarity, honesty, and competitive brilliance.
